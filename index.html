The Story

You are trapped inside an abandoned factory that should have been silent decades ago. But the walls still breathe. The machines still groan. And somewhere in the dark — they are waiting for you.

Four factory workers, corrupted by greed and destroyed by the very system they served, now haunt these halls as vengeful ghosts. Each one carries the weight of their sins: the foreman who covered up deaths, the accountant who falsified records, the manager who took bribes, and the inspector who looked away.

ASHUCHI — meaning "impure" — is both their curse and their identity. But hidden within the nightmare is a darker truth: their guilt is real, and so is the possibility of their redemption.

Survive the night. Learn the truth. And decide — do they deserve to be free?

Good Ending: In the SHUCHI path — the pure ending — all four ghosts find redemption. The title itself transforms in meaning: from Ashuchi (impure) to Shuchi (pure). The factory falls silent at last.

Who We Are

WE ARE
NINE
ACT

NineAct Games is an independent game studio based in Jaipur, India. We are storytellers first — every mechanic, every system, every line of dialogue serves the emotional truth of the game.

We believe that horror is at its most powerful when it is personal. When the fear comes not from what jumps out of the darkness, but from what the darkness reveals about the world — and about you.

We are building ASHUCHI: Curse of Greed — a psychological horror game that asks a simple, devastating question: can the guilty ever truly be forgiven?

Made in Unreal Engine 5. Made in India. Made with intention.
